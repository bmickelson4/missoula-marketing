Brennan Mickelson

MS, CSCS, RSCC

Cincinnati Reds — MiLB Strength & Conditioning Coach
National Strength & Conditioning Association (NSCA) — Montana State Director

Over a decade of coaching experience across every level — general population, weekend warriors, Division I collegiate, and professional athletes — including multiple MLB All-Stars in seasons before their selection.

Now in his 8th season of professional baseball as a Strength & Conditioning Coach: six seasons with the Seattle Mariners and the last two with the Cincinnati Reds.

Brennan holds an MS in Health and Human Performance from the University of Montana (2019) and a BS in Exercise Science with a minor in Strength & Conditioning from Washington State University (2017). He began his professional coaching career as the AZL Strength Coach for the Mariners in 2017.

Brennan lives in Missoula with his wife, Chloe, and their twin boys.

Achievements: 2021 High-A West Strength Coach of the Year · PBSCCS Presenter (2026)

Certifications: NSCA-CSCS · NSCA-RSCC · TPI Level I + Medical Level II · FMS Level I + II · Multi-Directional Speed Instructor · CPR / First Aid
